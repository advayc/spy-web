Spy — Party Game

A modern, mobile-friendly social deduction game. Play classic "Guess the Spy" or the new Range Mode with friends in seconds.

Play on Web How it works Get the App

Two Game Modes

• Guess the Spy: 1 spy, everyone else shares a topic. Talk, deduce, vote.
• Range Mode: 1 player gets a hidden range, others get the same prompt. Stay in range to win.

Quick Start

No accounts. Add players, pick a topic, and start. Perfect for living rooms, classrooms, or calls.

Hundreds of Prompts

Curated topics and 400+ range prompts for endless replayability. Create your own topics, too.
