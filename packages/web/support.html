Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play Spy?
    Check the main page for rules and gameplay videos. Each round, one or more players are secretly assigned as spies. The rest must deduce who the spies are before time runs out.
  • Can I create custom topics?
    Yes! Go to the topics section in the app to add your own word lists and categories.
  • Is Spy available on iOS and Android?
    Yes, you can download it from the App Store or Google Play.
  • How do I report a bug?
    Open an issue on GitHub or email us directly.

Troubleshooting

  • App won’t start? Try restarting your device and ensure you have the latest version installed.
  • Problems with multiplayer? Make sure all players are on the same Wi-Fi or have a stable internet connection.
  • Other issues? Contact us via email or GitHub for personalized help.
